The 3-Step Action

⛽

Don’t Top Off

Wait longer before refueling when safe. Avoid buying early just to keep the tank full.

📉

Buy What You Need

Keep less extra fuel sitting unused in your tank. Don’t store their profits for them.

🔥

Burn Less When You Can

Combine trips, reduce idling, carpool once, slow down, or skip one unnecessary drive.

Why This Matters

Every extra gallon sitting in millions of tanks has already been paid for. When we stop storing excess fuel in our tanks and reduce how much we burn, we create demand pressure together.

This is not about blaming working people who have to drive. This is about coordinated action: small changes, repeated by many people, becoming visible pressure.
